# Omahi Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 5, 2026_

Omahi is a cycle-aware planning browser extension. This policy is short because
the honest answer is short: **your data never leaves your device.**

## What Omahi stores

- Your cycle configuration: last period start date, cycle length, period length
- Period start dates you log
- Your preferences (for example, whether the new-tab page is enabled)

All of it is stored locally in your browser via `chrome.storage.local`.

## What Omahi does NOT do

- No servers. Omahi makes no network requests; nothing is uploaded, anywhere.
- No accounts. There is nothing to sign into and no identity attached to your data.
- No analytics, tracking, ads, or third-party SDKs.
- No selling or sharing of data — Omahi has nothing to sell or share.

## Your control

- **Export** your data any time as a JSON file from Settings.
- **Import** a backup to restore it.
- **Delete all data** from Settings; removal is immediate and complete.
  Uninstalling the extension also removes everything.

## A note on health information

Cycle dates are sensitive. That sensitivity is exactly why Omahi is local-only
by design. Omahi's phase estimates are heuristics and its suggestions are
lifestyle ideas — never medical advice.
